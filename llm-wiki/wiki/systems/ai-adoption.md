# System Variable — AI Contract Review Adoption Rate (stock)

The central stock of the CLD. It sits at the intersection of two feedback loops → a leverage point.

- **R loop (cost)**: adoption↑ → review cost↓ → adoption↑ (virtuous cycle). [[systems/review-cost]]
- **B loop (liability)**: adoption↑ → liability disputes↑ ⊣ adoption (self-limiting). [[systems/liability-disputes]]

> The [[drivers/ai-contract-review]] strength in the operational layer serves as a proxy observation for this variable.
